About Us

Zimro is an online delivery service which is based in Porur, Chennai. Tamil Peoples love for food is the driving force behind this venture. Started as a food delivery platform in 2019, we bring all the restaurants under one platform which provides the customers with variety of choices. We connect customers with many local restaurants online, allowing customers to view menus and place orders for delivery in a quick, fun, friendly and hassle-free way. Customers can order food and access a wide range of discounts using our website andmobile application (IOS/ANDROID) in a few clicks.
